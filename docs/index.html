What you own

Yours. Not rented.
Not locked in.

Most AI tools rent you access to someone else's model running on someone else's servers. Numin is the opposite. You own the runtime, you own the data, and you can leave anytime.

Your Mac

On Local, your agent runs on your physical hardware. On Cloud, your agent runs on a dedicated cloud Mac that is yours alone. Either way, you own the runtime — not a shared instance.

Your data

Your business data, your conversations, your memory — all of it stays on your machine (or your cloud Mac). We never train anyone else's model on your data. We can't, because we never have access to it.

Your agent

The skills, integrations, and customizations your agent learns are yours. Cancel your subscription tomorrow and your agent keeps running on your Mac. The knowledge doesn't disappear.

No lock-in

No proprietary data formats. No proprietary APIs. Your agent talks to your tools through standard protocols. Switch LLMs, switch channels, switch deployment — all without losing anything.

Open architecture

Built on an open agentic platform. Your codebase, your integration points, your documentation. You can read it, modify it, or hire someone else to extend it.

Portable

Take your agent and move it anywhere. From your Mac to your cloud Mac, from one cloud provider to another, from one LLM to another. It's your agent. It goes where you go.
