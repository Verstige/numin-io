# Munro Refund Policy

_Last updated: July 14, 2026_

## 30-day money-back guarantee (first-time customers)

If you are a new customer and you cancel within 30 days of your first payment, we refund the setup fee and the first month's subscription in full. No questions asked.

Email julylan.johnson@gmail.com to request a refund.

## After 30 days

After the 30-day window, monthly subscription fees are non-refundable. You can cancel at any time and the cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep access until the period ends.

## Setup fees

Setup fees are refundable within 30 days of purchase, regardless of whether the service has been activated.

## Service not delivered

If we have not provisioned your agent within 14 days of your setup fee being paid, you may request a full refund and we will process it within 5 business days.

## How to request a refund

Email julylan.johnson@gmail.com with:
- The email address on your account
- The reason (optional — helps us improve)

We will respond within 1 business day. Approved refunds post to your card within 5–10 business days.

## Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your card issuer without first contacting us for a refund, we may suspend your account while we investigate. We'd rather refund you directly — please reach out first.
