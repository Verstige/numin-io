# Munro Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 14, 2026_

## What we collect

When you sign up: your name, email, billing info (handled by Stripe — we do not store your card).
When you use the service: messages you send to your agent, files you upload, integrations you connect (e.g. Gmail, Calendar), and any tasks your agent performs on your behalf.
Operational: usage logs, error logs, billing events.

## What we do with it

- Run your agent service
- Troubleshoot issues you ask us to fix
- Improve the service (anonymized, aggregated only)
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your messages to train models without your explicit consent.

## Where it's stored

Your agent runs on a dedicated virtual computer ("your box") provisioned on Orgo infrastructure. Your data stays on your box. Operational logs (timestamps, errors) are stored in our system for 90 days then deleted.

## Your controls

- Export: download all your data from your dashboard anytime.
- Delete: request deletion; we delete within 30 days.
- Pause: pause the service without deleting; your data is preserved in cold storage until you resume.
- Channel revoke: disconnect any integration from your dashboard; we revoke the OAuth token immediately.

## Subprocessors

We use the following subprocessors (third parties that may process your data on our behalf):

- **Orgo** — host infrastructure (your box runs here)
- **Stripe** — billing and payment processing
- **Telegram / Discord / WhatsApp / iMessage gateways** — the channels you connect
- **Google Workspace APIs** — if you connect Gmail/Calendar/Drive

Each operates under its own data-protection terms. We have data processing agreements with each.

## International transfers

If you are outside the US, your data may be processed in the US. By using the service, you authorize this transfer.
